üöÄ Getting Started

Adding your first medication is easy:

  • Open the DoseUp app
  • Tap the "+" button on the Today screen or go to the Medications tab
  • Enter your medication name (you can type or search from our database)
  • Add the dosage (amount and unit, e.g., "10 mg")
  • Set your reminder times for each day
  • Add any notes or special instructions
  • Tap "Save" to create your medication

Your medication will now appear on your Today screen with scheduled reminders!

No account is required for basic features! DoseUp works immediately after download:

  • Free Features: Add up to 3 medications, set reminders, track basic adherence
  • Premium Features: Require subscription through Apple's App Store (no separate account needed)
  • Your Data: Stored locally on your device for maximum privacy
  • Cloud Sync: Premium users can optionally sync across devices using Apple ID

DoseUp will ask for notification permission when you first open the app. If you need to enable them later:

  • Go to iPhone Settings > DoseUp > Notifications
  • Turn on "Allow Notifications"
  • Choose your preferred alert style (Banners, Alerts, etc.)
  • Enable sounds and badges if desired
  • Return to DoseUp and verify notifications are working in Settings

üíä Managing Medications

Yes! DoseUp supports flexible scheduling:

  • When adding or editing a medication, tap on each day of the week
  • Set custom times for each day (e.g., 8 AM on weekdays, 9 AM on weekends)
  • Skip days entirely if you don't take the medication that day
  • Set multiple doses per day with different intervals
  • Use "Every X hours" for medications that need precise timing

To modify your medications:

  • Edit: Go to Medications tab, tap on the medication name, then tap "Edit"
  • Quick Edit: Long press on a medication in the Today view for quick options
  • Delete: In the medication details, scroll down and tap "Delete Medication"
  • Deactivate: Toggle off "Active" to pause without deleting history

Note: Deleting a medication removes all associated dose history. Consider deactivating instead if you might restart it later.

DoseUp helps you handle missed doses:

  • Visual Indication: Missed doses appear in red on your Today screen
  • Options: Mark as "Taken Late", "Skipped", or "Missed"
  • Late Doses: You can still mark doses as taken after the scheduled time
  • Analytics: Premium users see patterns in missed doses to improve adherence
  • Notifications: Overdue dose reminders (if enabled) will continue until marked

For PRN (as needed) medications:

  • When adding the medication, select "As Needed" instead of setting fixed times
  • The medication will appear on your Today screen without scheduled times
  • Tap "Take Now" whenever you use the medication
  • Set maximum daily limits to prevent overuse
  • Track frequency and patterns in your analytics

üîî Notifications & Reminders

Check these common issues:

  • Permissions: Settings > DoseUp > Notifications must be enabled
  • Do Not Disturb: Check if DND is blocking notifications
  • Focus Modes: Ensure DoseUp is allowed in active Focus modes
  • Notification Style: Try changing from banners to alerts
  • Sounds: Enable notification sounds in DoseUp Settings
  • Background Refresh: Enable in Settings > General > Background App Refresh

Yes, you can personalize your reminder sounds:

  • Go to DoseUp Settings > Notifications
  • Choose from built-in medication reminder sounds
  • Select different sounds for different types of reminders
  • Set vibration patterns for silent reminders
  • Premium users can set custom sounds per medication

Snoozing reminders gives you extra time:

  • From Notification: Swipe and tap "Snooze" (default 15 minutes)
  • From App: Tap the snooze button next to any due medication
  • Custom Duration: Long press to choose 5, 10, 15, 30, or 60 minutes
  • Settings: Change default snooze duration in Settings

‚≠ê Premium Features

DoseUp Plus unlocks powerful features:

  • Unlimited Medications: Add as many medications as needed
  • Advanced Analytics: Detailed charts, trends, and adherence insights
  • Intelligent Scheduling: Drug interaction warnings and optimal timing suggestions
  • Family Sharing: Share schedules with caregivers and family members
  • Data Export: Generate reports for doctors, export to HealthKit
  • Cloud Sync: Secure backup and sync across all your devices
  • Custom Themes: Personalize your app appearance

Family sharing helps caregivers stay informed (Premium feature):

  • Go to Settings > Family Sharing
  • Tap "Add Family Member" and enter their email
  • Choose permission level: View Only, Notifications, or Full Access
  • Send them the sharing invitation
  • They'll receive access through the DoseUp app or email updates
  • You can revoke access anytime

Create professional medical reports (Premium feature):

  • Go to Analytics tab > Export Report
  • Choose date range (last 30 days, 3 months, or custom)
  • Select format: PDF for doctors, CSV for analysis
  • Include adherence statistics, missed doses, and patterns
  • Share via email, print, or save to Files

Subscriptions are managed through Apple:

  • Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID (your name at top)
  • Tap "Subscriptions"
  • Find "DoseUp Plus" and tap it
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription"
  • You'll keep premium features until the end of your billing period
  • Your data remains safely stored and accessible

üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Force Close: Double-tap home button, swipe up on DoseUp to close
  • Restart: Reopen the app
  • Restart Device: Turn your iPhone off and on
  • Update App: Check App Store for updates
  • Free Storage: Ensure you have at least 1GB free space
  • iOS Version: Update to the latest iOS version

Don't panic - your data is likely still there:

  • First: Restart the app completely
  • Wait: Sometimes data takes a moment to load after updates
  • Check iCloud: If you had cloud sync enabled, data may need to re-sync
  • Premium Users: Go to Settings > Data Management > Restore from Backup
  • Still Missing: Contact support immediately with your device model and iOS version

To restore premium features:

  • Go to Settings > Premium > Restore Purchase
  • Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase
  • Wait for verification (this can take a few minutes)
  • If it fails, restart the app and try again
  • Check your Apple ID subscription status in iPhone Settings
  • Contact Apple Support if the subscription isn't showing in Apple's system

üîê Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority:

  • No Tracking: We don't collect usage analytics or personal information
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using iOS security features
  • Biometric Protection: Optional Face ID/Touch ID app lock
  • Cloud Sync: End-to-end encrypted when using premium cloud features

Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Yes, your data belongs to you:

  • Premium Users: Full data export in multiple formats (JSON, CSV, PDF)

  • When: Export anytime, even after canceling premium

  • Complete: Delete the app from your device

  • Confirmation: This action cannot be undone
  • Recommendation: Export your data first as a backup
