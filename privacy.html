Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is fundamental to everything we do. DoseUp is designed from the ground up to protect your personal health information while providing you with powerful medication management features.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Medication Information: Names, dosages, schedules, and notes you enter
  • Profile Information: Optional name and email for app personalization
  • Premium Account Data: Subscription status and payment information (processed by Apple)

Information We Don't Collect

  • We don't track your app usage patterns
  • We don't collect location data
  • We don't access your contacts or photos
  • We don't use third-party analytics or advertising networks

How We Use Your Information

Your medication data is used exclusively to provide app functionality:

  • Sending you medication reminders
  • Tracking your adherence progress
  • Generating reports and analytics
  • Syncing data across your devices (premium users only)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. This data is protected by your device's security features, including encryption and biometric authentication.

Cloud Sync (Premium Feature)

Premium users can optionally enable cloud synchronization:

  • Data is encrypted end-to-end before leaving your device
  • We use Apple's CloudKit infrastructure for maximum security
  • Only you can decrypt and access your synced data
  • You can disable cloud sync at any time

Security Measures

  • All data transmissions use SSL/TLS encryption
  • We employ industry-standard security practices
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Minimal server-side data storage

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health information with third parties. The only exceptions are:

  • With Your Explicit Consent: When you choose to share reports with healthcare providers
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Service Providers: Limited technical data with trusted partners who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights and Controls

Data Access and Portability

  • Export all your data at any time through the app
  • View and modify your information within the app
  • Request a copy of any data we have about you

Data Deletion

  • Delete individual medications, doses, or data entries
  • Clear all data through the app's settings
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting us
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

Communication Preferences

  • Control all notification settings within the app
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time
  • Manage subscription-related emails through your Apple ID

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

We use Apple's services for:

  • App Store: App distribution and subscription management
  • CloudKit: Optional cloud sync for premium users
  • HealthKit: Optional health data integration (with your permission)

No Third-Party Analytics

Unlike many apps, we don't use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or social media integrations that could track your behavior.

Children's Privacy

DoseUp is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

DoseUp is primarily designed for users in regions where Apple's App Store is available. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where we operate, subject to applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will:

  • Notify you of significant changes through the app
  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

Medical Information Disclaimer

Important: DoseUp is a medication reminder tool and is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers regarding your medical conditions and treatments. Your use of this app does not create a doctor-patient relationship.
